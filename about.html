Discover Alisar Dead Sea Products Factory

Alisar for Dead Sea Products was established in 2009 as a private limited liability company. The company’s main activity focuses on high-quality skincare and cosmetic products derived from Dead Sea minerals.

The Board of Directors are major investors with vast experience in the Dead Sea products industry, ensuring growth and international recognition.

Our Story

2009

Founding of ALISAR Dead Sea Products. Launch of the first skincare line.

2010

Introducing new natural cosmetics products inspired by Dead Sea minerals.

2012

Expansion of product range and gaining first international customers.

2016

Implemented ISO standards for high-quality skincare production.

2017

Building our own factory. First production starts in July.

2019

Celebrating ALISAR’s 10th anniversary.

2023

Launching a new skincare brand “ALISAR Body & Hair” and new testing projects.

2024

15 years together – celebrating the 15th anniversary of ALISAR.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Vision

To be recognized globally as a leader in Dead Sea skincare, delivering innovation, quality, and trust to every customer.

Mr. Atef Idais

Founder & CEO

Mr. Atef Idais is the founder and guiding force behind Alisar, bringing over 15 years of experience in the Dead Sea products industry. With a vision to combine traditional natural remedies with modern cosmetic science, he ensures that every product is safe, effective, and of high quality. His dedication to sustainability and excellence reflects in all aspects of Alisar’s offerings, making the brand trusted and respected by customers.

Eng.Rose Al-Maaitah

Quality Manager

Eng.Rose Al-Maaitah stands at the forefront of Alisar’s pursuit of excellence, embodying precision, dedication, and an unwavering commitment to quality. As the driving force behind the Quality Assurance department, she meticulously oversees every stage of production, from the careful inspection of raw materials to the rigorous testing of finished products. Her leadership ensures that all Alisar offerings not only comply with, but often surpass, international ISO and GMP standards, guaranteeing the highest level of safety, efficacy, and customer satisfaction.

Omar Alhadidi

Maintenance Manager

Eng.Omar is responsible for overseeing all maintenance and operational processes at Alisar, ensuring smooth production and optimal performance of equipment. With his expertise in chemical engineering, he ensures that every system runs efficiently, safely, and in full compliance with international standards, supporting the consistent quality of Alisar’s products.
