Our Services

We transform your vision into high-quality, beautifully formulated personal care products.

Consultation & Formulation

The first step in developing a private label product at Alisar begins with understanding your vision. We take the time to listen, explore ideas, and define the product concept that truly reflects your brand identity and market positioning. During this phase, our specialists support you in shaping the formulation, selecting high-quality ingredients, and ensuring the product aligns with performance and regulatory standards. Our goal is to create a product that is both effective and memorable, delivering real value to your target customers.

This phase includes:

  • Understanding your product vision and target market.
  • Choosing formulas, textures, active ingredients, and fragrance direction.
  • Prototype development and product performance testing.
  • Packaging selection & branding alignment.
  • Determining production quantities and future scaling plans.
  • Cost planning based on formula, packaging, and quantities.
  • Setting a clear production and launch timeline.

Designing & Registration

At this stage, our team assists in bringing your product identity to life. We work closely with you to develop a design direction that reflects your brand values through packaging style, label layout, product narrative, and overall aesthetic presentation. In parallel, we guide you through the required regulatory steps to ensure your product complies with cosmetic standards and market regulations — allowing you to launch confidently and professionally.

Manufacturing

At Alisar, we follow a high-standard manufacturing process supported by our GMP certification. Our production facility is equipped to produce skincare and personal care products with precision, safety, and consistency. Every formula is prepared using high-quality raw materials, carefully measured and mixed under controlled conditions to ensure product stability and effectiveness.

Our production lines are supported by advanced filling, packaging, and sealing technologies, ensuring every item is delivered with the highest level of care — from mixing to the final packaged product.

Our facility provides:

  • GMP-certified production environment.
  • Modern machinery for liquid and cream formulations.
  • Strict hygiene and safety procedures.
  • Quality checks at every stage of production.
  • Professional filling, labeling, and packaging processes.

Packaging

At Alisar, packaging is more than just a container — it's a reflection of your brand identity. We carefully select packaging that aligns with your brand’s visual character, targeted audience, and market positioning. Our team assists you in choosing high-quality bottles, jars, labels, and finishing details that enhance product appeal and ensure a premium customer experience.

We collaborate with professional printing houses and trusted suppliers to guarantee durability, consistency, and aesthetic elegance in every final product.

Our packaging support includes:

  • Selection of bottles, jars, pumps, and tubes suitable for skincare formulations.
  • Label design guidance and brand identity alignment.
  • Options for matte, glossy, frosted, and eco-friendly finishes.
  • Primary and secondary packaging customization.
  • Coordinating printing, label application, and final presentation.

Product Testing

At Alisar, every product undergoes a carefully structured testing process to ensure safety, stability, and performance. Our in-house laboratory is equipped to evaluate each formula during and after production, allowing us to verify ingredient integrity, texture consistency, preservation effectiveness, and skin compatibility. These testing procedures follow international quality and regulatory guidelines, ensuring that each product meets professional standards and is ready for distribution in both local and global markets.

Delivery

Once your products are fully manufactured and packaged, they are prepared for delivery with precision and care. Whether you are shipping locally or internationally, we coordinate with trusted logistics partners to ensure safe, timely, and organized delivery. Our goal is to ensure that every product arrives in perfect condition — ready to represent your brand with confidence.
